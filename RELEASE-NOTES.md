## Privacy and security handling

- Images are web-optimised as WebP files for faster loading.
- Key cuts, key identifiers and key-safe internal details have been cropped or obscured where appropriate.
- The copy does not attach a customer review to a photograph unless the job relationship is known.

## Future photo workflow

Supply each new image with the general area, service, problem, work completed, useful result and any matching genuine review. This allows it to be used accurately in Recent Work, Case Studies, relevant service pages and the gallery.
